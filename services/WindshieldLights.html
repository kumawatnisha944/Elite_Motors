FAQ's

Why is windshield inspection important?
The windshield protects you from dust, wind and road debris. Cracks or chips can reduce visibility and may become dangerous while driving. Regular inspection helps prevent accidents and costly replacement.
What problems occur if car lights are not working properly?
Faulty headlights, indicators or brake lights reduce night visibility and increase accident risk. Proper lighting ensures you can see the road clearly and other drivers can notice your vehicle.
Do you repair windshield cracks or replace the glass?
Small chips and minor cracks can be repaired, but if the damage is large or in the driver‚Äôs view area, complete windshield replacement is recommended for safety.
Do you check all car lights during service?
Yes. We inspect headlights, fog lamps, tail lights, indicators, brake lights and interior lights. We also replace fused bulbs and fix wiring issues if needed.
How often should windshield and lights be checked?
It is recommended to inspect windshield and lights every 3 to 6 months or before long trips and monsoon season to ensure safe and clear driving.
