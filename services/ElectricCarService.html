What is Electric Car Service?

Electric car service is the periodic maintenance required to keep your EV running safely and efficiently. Unlike petrol or diesel cars, electric vehicles do not require engine oil changes, but they still need regular inspection of the battery pack, cooling system, charging port, electric motor, brakes, tyres and software systems. Generally, electric cars should be serviced every 8,000 to 12,000 km or at least once a year as recommended by the manufacturer. Regular servicing helps maintain battery performance, driving range and overall vehicle safety.

FAQ's

Do electric cars also need servicing?
Yes. Even though electric cars have fewer moving parts than petrol or diesel vehicles, they still require regular servicing to check battery health, cooling system, brakes, suspension and software updates.
What is checked during an electric car service?
Electric car service includes battery inspection, charging system check, motor condition, brake system, tyre condition, coolant level and diagnostic scanning of electronic systems.
Is electric car servicing expensive?
No. Electric car servicing is usually cheaper than petrol or diesel cars because there is no engine oil, clutch or gearbox maintenance required.
How often should an electric car be serviced?
It is recommended to service your electric car every 8,000 to 12,000 km or once a year to maintain battery efficiency and vehicle safety.
Can I charge my electric car after servicing?
Yes. After servicing, the charging system is tested and verified. You can safely charge and drive your electric car without any issues.
