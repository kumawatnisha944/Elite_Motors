What is CNG Fitting & Servicing?

CNG fitting is the process of installing a certified CNG kit in your petrol car to allow it to run on compressed natural gas. It includes cylinder installation, pressure regulator setup, proper wiring, and safety testing. Regular CNG servicing is equally important to maintain correct gas flow, mileage and engine performance. Generally, a CNG kit should be inspected and serviced every 8,000 to 10,000 km to ensure safe driving, prevent gas leakage and keep the vehicle running efficiently.
