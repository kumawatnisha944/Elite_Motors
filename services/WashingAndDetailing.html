What is Car Washing & Detailing?

Car washing and detailing is a professional cleaning and protection service designed to maintain the appearance and condition of your vehicle. Car washing includes exterior cleaning using pressure water and automotive shampoo to safely remove dust, mud and road contaminants. Car detailing is a deeper process that involves interior vacuum cleaning, dashboard polishing, seat cleaning, tyre dressing and paint protection. Regular washing and detailing not only improves the shine of your car but also protects the paint, prevents rust and keeps the vehicle hygienic and well-maintained.

Benefits of Car Washing & Detailing

  • Protects car paint from dust, mud and harmful contaminants
  • Restores original shine and glossy finish of the vehicle
  • Prevents rust and paint damage caused by dirt and moisture
  • Keeps interior clean, hygienic and odor-free
  • Improves overall appearance and increases resale value

FAQ's

What is the difference between car wash and car detailing?
Car wash is a basic exterior cleaning, while car detailing is a deep cleaning process that includes interior cleaning, polishing and protection of your vehicle.
How often should I wash my car?
It is recommended to wash your car at least once every week to remove dust, mud and harmful particles that can damage the paint.
Is car shampoo safe for my vehicle paint?
Yes, we use only automotive-grade car shampoo which is specially designed to clean your car without harming the paint surface.
Will polishing damage my car paint?
No. Professional polishing actually protects your paint, removes minor scratches and restores the shine of your car.
How much time is required for car wash or detailing service?
Normally it takes around 1 to 2 hours depending on the service package and condition of the vehicle.
