What is Paint Protection Coating?

Paint Protection Coating (PPC) is a protective layer applied on the exterior surface of your car to safeguard the original paint. A special chemical coating is carefully applied after deep cleaning and polishing so it bonds with the paint surface and forms a transparent shield. This coating protects the car from UV rays, dust, water spots, bird droppings, oxidation and minor scratches while maintaining a deep glossy shine and smooth finish.

Benefits of Paint Protection Coating

  • Protects car paint from UV rays and sun fading
  • Prevents minor scratches, swirl marks and dust damage
  • Maintains long-lasting shine and glossy showroom finish
  • Repels water, stains and bird droppings (hydrophobic effect)
  • Increases resale value of the vehicle

FAQ's

What is Paint Protection Coating (PPC)?
Paint Protection Coating is a protective chemical layer applied on your car‚Äôs exterior paint. It protects the car from scratches, sun damage, dust, water spots and fading while maintaining a glossy showroom finish.
How long does paint protection coating last?
Normally the coating lasts between 1 to 3 years depending on maintenance, driving conditions and washing methods used.
Does coating protect the car from scratches?
Yes, it protects from minor scratches, swirl marks and daily wear. However, it cannot protect from major accidents or deep scratches caused by heavy impact.
How much time is required for coating service?
Paint protection coating usually takes 6 to 8 hours depending on car size and paint condition because proper cleaning and surface preparation is required.
How should I maintain my car after coating?
Avoid washing the car for 2 to 3 days after coating. Later, use soft cloth washing and mild shampoo only. Do not use harsh chemicals or hard brushes to maintain long-lasting shine.
