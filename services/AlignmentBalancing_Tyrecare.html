What is Wheel Alignment & Balancing?

Wheel alignment and balancing is an important maintenance service that keeps your car stable, safe and smooth while driving. In wheel alignment, the angles of the tyres are adjusted according to the manufacturer‚Äôs specifications so the car moves straight and the steering remains centered. Wheel balancing ensures that the weight of the tyres and wheels is evenly distributed to avoid vibration at high speed. It is generally recommended to check alignment and balancing every 5,000 km or whenever you notice steering pulling, uneven tyre wear or vibration while driving.
