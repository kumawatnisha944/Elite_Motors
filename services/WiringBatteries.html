What is Wiring & Battery Service?

Wiring & Battery Service is an important electrical maintenance process of your car. In this service, the battery condition, terminals, alternator charging, fuses and complete wiring connections are checked and repaired if required. Over time, battery performance decreases and wiring connections may become loose, corroded or damaged, which can cause starting problems and electrical failures. Regular inspection helps prevent sudden breakdowns, battery drain and malfunction of lights, horn, sensors and electronic accessories. It is recommended to check your car battery and wiring system periodically, especially before long drives or seasonal weather changes, to keep your vehicle reliable and safe.

FAQ's

What problems occur due to faulty car wiring?
Faulty wiring can cause headlights failure, horn not working, power window issues, battery drain and sometimes the car may not start at all.
How do I know my car battery is weak?
If your car takes time to start, headlights become dim, or you need frequent jump-starts, it usually means your battery is weak and needs testing or replacement.
How long does a car battery usually last?
Normally a car battery lasts between 2 to 4 years depending on usage, weather conditions and vehicle maintenance.
Do you repair wiring issues or only replace parts?
At Elite Motors Jaipur, our technicians first inspect and repair wiring wherever possible. Parts are replaced only if they are completely damaged.
