What is Tune Up or General Service of Car?

ECM Scanning & Repair is a computerized diagnostic process used to check the electronic brain of your car, known as the Engine Control Module (ECU/ECM). Modern vehicles depend on sensors and electronic controls to manage fuel injection, ignition timing, air-fuel ratio and engine performance. Using advanced diagnostic scanners, technicians read fault codes, identify sensor failures, wiring issues or software errors and repair them accurately.
